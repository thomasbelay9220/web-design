Oromo Musics

Oromos use music to "remember" past histories, bolster a sense of community among Oromo speaking groups. during the emperor Haile Selassie regime Afan Oromo was banned from being spoken, taught, or administratively used in the country in order to subdue the Oromo people and culture in 1941. Oromo music is used by the current regime in Ethiopia to present a face of multiculturalism.
